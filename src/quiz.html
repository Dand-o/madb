Misinformation Online.
What is misinformation? Misinformation online, is false information that is spread by people who do not realise that this information is false. It's important to note, this different from what we hear as 'fake news' and disinformation. Fake news refers to websites or srouces that delibrately share or create misleading information.
What We Do.
Our aim is to educate and help you identify misinformation online. This includes social media platforms and news sources. By providing an educational resources to help people understand and interpret misinformation, we hope to help prevent the spread of it and help you identify reliable and trustworthy news sources and articles.
